# Chinook SQL Portfolio Project

## Overview
This project showcases my **SQL skills** using the popular Chinook sample database, which simulates a digital music store. The goal of this project is to demonstrate practical SQL ability across a range of topics including joins, aggregations, window functions, CTEs, and analytical problem-solving.

---

Across this project, I demonstrate proficiency in:

- Basic querying with **SELECT, WHERE, ORDER BY, LIMIT**  
- Inner and outer joins (**JOIN, LEFT JOIN**)  
- Aggregations using **COUNT, SUM, AVG**  
- Grouping with **GROUP BY** and filtering with **HAVING**  
- Common Table Expressions (**CTEs**)

## Business Questions & Insights

1. **Which tracks have a composer listed?**  
   *Insight:* Evaluates data completeness for the composer field, enabling composer-based analysis and reporting.

2. **What are the highest-valued invoices?**  
   *Insight:* Identifies the largest purchases, highlighting highly engaged or high-value customers.

3. **How many tracks are in each genre?**  
   *Insight:* Shows catalog distribution across genres, highlighting where most content is concentrated.

4. **Which customers have spent the most money?**  
   *Insight:* Highlights top lifetime customers, ideal for retention and loyalty initiatives.

5. **Which artists have the most tracks?**  
   *Insight:* Identifies artists with the largest catalog presence, potentially correlating with visibility and sales volume.

6. **Which customers spend more than the average?**  
   *Insight:* Separates above-average spenders from the broader customer base to identify high-value users.

7. **Rank customers by total spending per country**  
   *Insight:* Reveals top customers within each country and supports region-specific business strategies.

8. **Which customers have never made a purchase?**  
   *Insight:* Identifies non-converted users for targeted engagement or onboarding campaigns.

9. **Which artists have more than 20 tracks?**  
   *Insight:* Highlights highly represented artists who may drive larger portions of catalog engagement.

10. **What is the revenue by month?**  
    *Insight:* Shows revenue trends over time, useful for identifying seasonality or growth patterns.

11. **How does cumulative revenue grow over time?**  
    *Insight:* Demonstrates overall business growth using a running total, providing a long-term performance view.

12. **Who are the most valued customers?**  
    *Insight:* Segments customers into High, Medium, and Low value groups, a common real-world analytics technique.

13. **Which countries have the highest average order value?**  
    *Insight:* Identifies geographic markets where customers spend more per purchase.

14. **Top 3 tracks by revenue per genre**  
    *Insight:* Highlights top-performing tracks within each genre, offering a more nuanced view than global rankings.

15. **Monthly revenue by country**  
    *Insight:* Breaks revenue trends down by geography, allowing regional performance comparison.

16. **Most popular media types by number of tracks sold**  
    *Insight:* Shows which media formats generate the most sales, useful for product and platform decisions.
